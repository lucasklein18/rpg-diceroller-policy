Privacy Policy

App: RPG Dice Roller – Multi Dice

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RPG Dice Roller – Multi Dice ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

RPG Dice Roller – Multi Dice is a tabletop RPG utility designed to roll dice and manage roll history. The app may display advertisements in the free version and offer optional rewarded ads that grant temporary ad-free usage. A paid version may also be available which removes advertisements and unlocks additional features.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not require account creation.

a) Data stored locally on your device

  • Dice roll history
  • User preferences and settings
  • Temporary ad-free reward status
  • Review prompt state

This information is stored locally on the user's device and is used only to support app functionality.

b) Advertising data

The free version of the app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These services may automatically collect information including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostic or performance information

This information may be used by advertising partners to deliver ads, limit ad frequency, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

c) Purchase information

If the user purchases the paid version of the app, payment processing is handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store credit card or payment information.

d) App rating interactions

The app may occasionally prompt users to rate the application. Ratings are handled through Google Play services.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Provide the core dice rolling functionality
  • Store roll history and user preferences locally
  • Display advertisements in the free version
  • Enable rewarded ad features such as temporary ad-free usage
  • Support premium features and purchases
  • Improve app stability and performance

4. Advertising

Advertisements may appear in the free version of the app. Users may optionally watch a rewarded advertisement to receive a temporary ad-free experience.

Advertising providers may collect certain information to deliver and optimize advertisements. Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers for more information about their data practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Data may be processed by trusted third-party service providers when necessary to operate the app, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play services
  • Google Play billing services

6. Data Retention

Data stored locally remains on the user's device until it is removed by:

  • Clearing app data
  • Uninstalling the application
  • Resetting the device storage

Google AdMob may retain data according to their own policies.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, please contact us.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information handled by the app. However, no electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
